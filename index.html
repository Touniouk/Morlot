About Me

A life lived
outside.

It started with a single boulder problem in the woods outside Paris — a move that took me three hours and left me grinning for the rest of the day. That was ten years ago. Since then I've chased problems and trails across six countries, drawn equally to the raw puzzle of a boulder and the meditative rhythm of a long hike.

Bouldering taught me focus and patience. Hiking taught me to slow down and take it all in. Together they make the perfect rhythm — push hard on the rock, then walk it off through the landscape. That's the loop I keep coming back to, and it's the experience I love sharing with others.

Disciplines

What we do,
and how.

Bouldering

Short, intense problems on natural rock — no ropes, no gear, just you and the stone. I guide sessions at all levels, from first moves to sending your project.

Day Hikes

A single day on the trail, hand-picked for the best views and terrain. Perfect for clearing your head between sessions or as a standalone adventure.

Boulder & Hike

The full loop — a morning of problems at a local spot, then an afternoon trail to cool down and take in the landscape. My favourite kind of day out.

Beginner Sessions

Never touched a boulder? No problem. These sessions strip things back to the basics — footwork, balance, and building confidence on easy problems.

Trail Running

Swap the boulder pad for running shoes. I lead easy-to-moderate trail runs through some of the prettiest forest and mountain routes around Fontainebleau.

Weekend Trips

Two days, one destination. Travel to a new bouldering area or hiking spot, camp under the stars, and make the most of every hour on the rock and the trail.

How It Works

Joining a session
is simple.

01

Get in Touch

Drop me a message via email or Instagram to tell me what you're after — a bouldering session, a day hike, or a full boulder-and-hike combo. No experience needed to get started.

02

Pick a Spot

I'll suggest a location based on your level and what you're in the mood for. Want easy problems and a flat trail? A challenge and a ridge walk? I know the best spots for both.

03

Get Out There

The day arrives — we boulder, we hike, we enjoy the fresh air. I'll keep things safe and fun, whether you're topping out your first problem or working a V6.

04

Remember It

Every session has a story worth keeping. I snap photos along the way — the sends, the views, the good vibes — so you can relive the day long after you're back home.
